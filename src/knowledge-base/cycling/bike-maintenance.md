# Bicycle Maintenance for Adventure Riders

## Pre-Ride ABC Check
Perform this 2-minute check before every ride:

### A - Air
- Check tyre pressure with a gauge. Road bikes: 80-130 PSI. Mountain bikes: 30-50 PSI. Hybrid: 50-70 PSI.
- Inspect tyres for embedded glass, thorns, or cuts
- Spin each wheel to check for wobbles (indicates a buckled rim)

### B - Brakes
- Squeeze each brake lever -- it should not touch the handlebar
- Check brake pad thickness -- replace if less than 1 mm of pad material remains
- For disc brakes: listen for rubbing when spinning the wheel. Adjust caliper alignment if needed
- Ensure brake cables are not frayed or kinked

### C - Chain
- The chain should be clean and lightly lubricated (not dripping wet with lube)
- Check for chain stretch using a chain checker tool -- replace at 0.75% wear
- Shift through all gears smoothly. Clicking or skipping indicates cable tension adjustment needed
- Inspect derailleur hanger for bends (common after crashes or transport)

## On-Trail Repairs You Should Know
1. **Flat tyre**: Remove wheel, unseat tyre with levers, replace or patch tube, re-inflate with mini pump or CO2 cartridge
2. **Broken chain**: Use a chain breaker tool to remove damaged link, rejoin with a quick link
3. **Loose bolts**: Tighten with a multi-tool. Check handlebars, seat post, and bottle cage bolts
4. **Brake adjustment**: Barrel adjuster on the lever end can tighten sluggish brakes on the trail

## Essential Tools to Carry
- Mini pump or CO2 inflator with cartridges
- Tyre levers (at least 2)
- Spare inner tube (correct valve type: Presta or Schrader)
- Patch kit as backup
- Multi-tool with Allen keys (4, 5, 6 mm), Phillips screwdriver, chain breaker
- Quick-link for your chain size
- Small rag for handling greasy parts

## Post-Ride Care
- Wipe down the frame and chain after dusty or muddy rides
- Re-lube the chain every 100-200 km or after riding in rain
- Check for loose spokes by squeezing pairs -- they should feel evenly tensioned
- Store your bike in a dry area to prevent rust
