# Essential Camping Gear Checklist

## Shelter
- **Tent**: 3-season tent for most Indian conditions. Look for waterproof rating of 2000mm+. Recommended: Quechua MH100 (budget), Naturehike Cloud Up 2 (lightweight), Coleman Sundome (family).
- **Groundsheet/Footprint**: Protects your tent floor from punctures and moisture
- **Tarp**: Versatile for rain shelter over cooking area

## Sleep System
- **Sleeping Bag**: Match the temperature rating to your destination
  - Summer plains: 15C comfort rating
  - Moderate altitude (6,000-10,000 ft): 5C to 0C comfort
  - Winter/high altitude: -10C to -20C comfort
  - Recommended: Decathlon Forclaz Trek 500 (0C), Quechua MH500 (15C)
- **Sleeping Pad**: Insulates you from cold ground. Foam pads (R-value 2+) for budget; inflatable pads (R-value 3.5+) for comfort. Never skip this -- ground saps body heat fast.
- **Pillow**: Compressible camp pillow or stuff sack filled with clothes

## Lighting
- **Headlamp**: Hands-free is essential. Minimum 200 lumens. Petzl Tikkina or Decathlon OnNight 100
- **Spare batteries or USB charging cable**
- **Camp lantern**: For ambient tent lighting (optional but nice)

## Fire and Cooking
- **Camp stove**: Butane canister stoves (Jetboil Flash, Primus Lite+) for quick boiling. Carry enough fuel for your trip duration.
- **Lighter + waterproof matches**: Always carry both as backup
- **Cookset**: Lightweight pot, pan, utensils. Steel or titanium.
- **Water purification**: LifeStraw, Sawyer Mini filter, or Aquatabs (chlorine tablets). Boiling works but uses fuel.

## First Aid Kit
- Adhesive bandages, gauze, medical tape
- Antiseptic wipes and povidone-iodine solution
- Crepe bandage for sprains
- Paracetamol, ibuprofen, antihistamines
- ORS sachets
- Prescription medications (carry extras)
- Blister pads (Compeed or similar)
- Triangular bandage for slings
- Tweezers and safety pins

## Tools and Misc
- **Knife/multi-tool**: Victorinox Swiss Army Knife or Leatherman
- **Paracord**: 15 metres minimum -- useful for clotheslines, repairs, shelter building
- **Duct tape**: Wrap around a pencil to save space. Fixes almost anything.
- **Trash bags**: For waste and as emergency waterproofing
- **Sunscreen (SPF 50)** and **insect repellent**
