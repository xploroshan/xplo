# Injury Prevention for Runners

## Warm-Up Routine (5-10 Minutes Before Every Run)
Never start a run cold. A proper warm-up reduces injury risk significantly.

1. **Brisk walk**: 2-3 minutes to elevate heart rate
2. **Leg swings**: 10 each leg, forward-backward and side-to-side
3. **Hip circles**: 10 each direction
4. **High knees**: 20 steps
5. **Butt kicks**: 20 steps
6. **Walking lunges**: 10 total
7. **Ankle circles**: 10 each direction per foot

**Note**: Static stretching (holding stretches) is for AFTER the run, not before. Dynamic warm-ups are for before.

## Common Running Injuries

### Runner's Knee (Patellofemoral Pain)
- **Symptoms**: Pain around or behind the kneecap, worse going downhill or stairs
- **Prevention**: Strengthen quads and glutes (squats, wall sits). Avoid sudden mileage increases
- **Treatment**: Ice after runs, reduce mileage, knee-strengthening exercises

### Shin Splints
- **Symptoms**: Pain along the inner edge of the shinbone
- **Prevention**: Gradual mileage increases, proper shoes, calf strengthening
- **Treatment**: Rest, ice, calf stretches. If persistent, rule out stress fracture with a doctor

### Plantar Fasciitis
- **Symptoms**: Sharp heel pain, worst with first steps in the morning
- **Prevention**: Calf stretches, arch-strengthening exercises, proper footwear
- **Treatment**: Roll foot on a frozen water bottle, night splint, gradual return to running

### IT Band Syndrome
- **Symptoms**: Pain on the outside of the knee, typically starts 2-3 km into a run
- **Prevention**: Foam rolling the IT band and outer thigh, hip-strengthening exercises
- **Treatment**: Rest, foam rolling, glute strengthening (clamshells, side-lying leg raises)

## Shoe Selection
The right shoes prevent most running injuries.

- **Get fitted**: Visit a running store (Decathlon, Asics store) for gait analysis
- **Replace shoes** every 600-800 km. Track mileage in your running app
- **Cushioned shoes** (Asics Gel-Nimbus, Nike Pegasus) for beginners and long distances
- **Stability shoes** (Brooks Adrenaline, Asics GT-2000) if you overpronate
- **Break in new shoes** on short runs first -- never race in untested shoes
- Budget options: Decathlon Kiprun KS500 (Rs 3,000), Skechers GoRun (Rs 4,000)

## Rest Day Importance
- Take at least **1-2 complete rest days per week**
- Rest is when your muscles repair and grow stronger
- Follow the hard-easy principle: never do two intense runs back-to-back
- Sleep 7-8 hours -- growth hormone release during deep sleep is critical for recovery
- **Warning signs you need rest**: persistent fatigue, elevated resting heart rate, irritability, recurring minor injuries, declining performance despite training
